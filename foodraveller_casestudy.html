Foodraveller

Foodraveller is the community of foodies and travellers so we call them FOODRAVELLERS. This is the platform where foodravellers can share there experiences, they can share their stories and photos. One more important thing about foodravellers is users can create events and checkout all latest events which is happening around

my role

I led the design of Foodraveller for the Web and Mobile website. My responsibilities are -

  • To findout those area where foodies or food bloggers and travellers or travel bloggers are facing problem.
  • Talk to bloggers about their experiences.
  • Competitive Analysis.
  • Information architecture.
  • Brainstorming.
  • Wireframe & Prototype.
  • Microinteractions.
  • Usability testing.

pain points

Here are the the problems which i observed during my research -

  • Travellers and foodies are more interested in reading stuff from real travellers and foodies rather than professional writer who mostly write for promotional activities although they write interesting stories.
  • There are many website for foodies and travellers but it is not easy to access relavant information based on their need.
  • There is dearth of website which offer to create fully customize events for event organizer in different area.
